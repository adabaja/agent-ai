Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Ahmed AI Assistant is a personal, private tool. It does not collect, store, or share any user data with third parties.

Data Access

The assistant accesses Gmail and Google Calendar solely to perform actions requested by the authorized user. No data is stored on external servers.

OAuth Scopes

Google OAuth is used exclusively for the personal account of the app owner. No other users are granted access or have their data processed.

Data Retention

No personal data is logged or retained. All processing happens locally on the owner's hardware.

Contact

For questions, reach out via GitHub.
